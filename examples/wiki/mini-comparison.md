# Mini comparison

## Decision context

Should we stop at source-note capture or perform activation into synthesis pages?

| Dimension | Source-note only | Capture + activation | Notes |
|-----------|------------------|----------------------|-------|
| Provenance retention | High | High | both preserve source |
| Reusability | Low | High | activation routes into canonical pages |
| Discoverability | Medium | High | index/hub updates improve retrieval |

## Guidance

Default to capture + activation for material decisions; defer explicitly in the log only when scope/time requires it.
